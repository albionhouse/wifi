Safe to use?

Taylor Square WiFi is an unencrypted and open wireless network. It is not recommended for internet banking or sending and receiving sensitive correspondence.

Trouble connecting?

You can only use a package on the same device payment was made from; multi-use vouchers can be used by multiple devices simultaneously. Check you have no other devices currently connected.
